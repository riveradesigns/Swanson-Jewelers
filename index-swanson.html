About Swanson Jewelers

Swanson Jewelers is a family-owned jewelry store located on 6th Street, just off the Coles County Courthouse square in historic downtown Charleston, Illinois.

Our Story

Built on personal relationships and word-of-mouth, Swanson Jewelers focuses on helping each customer find or create pieces that feel truly personal, from engagement rings to meaningful gifts.

Customers consistently mention relaxed visits, no-pressure conversations, and staff who take extra time to explain options and work within a range of budgets.

What We Specialize In

  • Custom-made jewelry design and remounts.
  • Engagement rings and wedding bands.
  • Jewelry repair, sizing, and cleaning.
  • Watch batteries, sizing, and service.
  • Buying old, unwanted, or broken jewelry, coins, and watches.
  • Everyday fine jewelry for gifts and special occasions.

Why Locals Choose Us

Friendly, relaxed service

Jewelry & Watch Services

Whether you are celebrating a milestone, refreshing a cherished piece, or simply replacing a watch battery, Swanson Jewelers offers hands-on service and honest advice.

Custom Design

Work one-on-one to design engagement rings, pendants, and other custom pieces, using new materials or stones you already own.

Repairs & Restoration

From ring sizing to stone tightening and chain repair, your jewelry is inspected and repaired with care so it is ready to wear again.

Watches & Seiko
